About Mario

Super Mario is a platform game series created by Nintendo. First released in 1985, the series became iconic due to its gameplay, design, and unforgettable characters.

Mario's Legacy

Mario games revolutionized video gaming. It introduced side-scrolling adventure, power-ups, and engaging level design. Mario remains Nintendo's mascot to this day.

Fun Facts

  • Mario was originally called "Jumpman".
  • The first Mario game was in Donkey Kong (1981).
  • Mario has appeared in over 200 games.
